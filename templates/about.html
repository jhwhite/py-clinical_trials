About

The clinical trials on this site comes from clinicaltrials.gov.

To ensure we are in accordance with the Terms & Conditions of clinicaltrials.gov we do the following:

  • The page for each trial gives a link to the corresponding trial on clinicaltrials.gov.
  • The information on this site is updated daily.
  • Each trial lists the date that ClinicalTrials processed the data.
  • We do not make any modifications to the data.

We do not assert any proprietary rights to any portion of the database, or represent the database or any part thereof to anyone as other than a United States Government database.

We do not use any email addresses extracted from our database for marketing or other promotional purposes.
